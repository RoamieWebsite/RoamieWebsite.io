New York Package

Arrive in Manhattan and check into your chosen accommodation. Whether it's a luxury hotel in Midtown or a trendy boutique hotel in SoHo, find your perfect spot. Take a leisurely walk through Central Park and grab dinner at a nearby restaurant. Start your day at Times Square, the heart of the Theater District. Explore Broadway and catch a matinee show if you're inclined. Visit the iconic Rockefeller Center and take the elevator to the Top of the Rock for panoramic views. Explore St. Patrick's Cathedral and have dinner in Hell's Kitchen or the Garment District.

Spend the morning at the Metropolitan Museum of Art (the Met) or the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA). Afternoon stroll through Central Park. You can rent a rowboat at the Loeb Boathouse or simply enjoy the scenery. Have dinner in the Upper West Side.

Spend your last day shopping on Fifth Avenue, exploring the flagship stores and boutiques. Visit the High Line, a unique elevated park built on a former railway track. Have a farewell dinner in Chelsea or the Meatpacking District.
