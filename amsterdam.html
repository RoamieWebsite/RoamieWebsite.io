Amsterdam Package

Arrive in Amsterdam and check into your chosen accommodation, perhaps a cozy canal-side hotel. Start your trip with a relaxing canal cruise to get a feel for the city's layout and enjoy picturesque views. In the evening, explore the Jordaan district, known for its charming streets, boutiques, and cafes. Have dinner at a local Dutch eatery.

Visit the iconic Rijksmuseum in the morning to admire Dutch art and history, including masterpieces by Rembrandt and Vermeer. Head to the Van Gogh Museum to explore the life and works of the famous artist. After lunch, take a stroll or rent a bike to explore Vondelpark. Enjoy the greenery and maybe have a picnic. Dinner in Leidseplein or Rembrandtplein, bustling squares with plenty of dining options.

Take a ferry to Amsterdam North and explore the up-and-coming NDSM wharf area with its street art and creative vibe. Visit the Eye Filmmuseum for cinema enthusiasts. Wrap up your trip with a farewell dinner at a waterfront restaurant with a view of the city skyline.
