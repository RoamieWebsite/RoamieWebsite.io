Lapland Package

Arrive in Rovaniemi, the capital of Finnish Lapland. Check into a cozy log cabin or a unique glass igloo for a Northern Lights view. Explore the city, visit the Arctic Circle and Santa Claus Village.

Embark on a thrilling husky safari through the snowy forests. Experience the exhilarating ride with these friendly dogs. In the evening, join a Northern Lights tour. Head to a dark spot away from city lights for the best chance to witness the auroras.

Visit a traditional reindeer farm. Enjoy a reindeer sleigh ride and learn about Sami culture. Check out the Snow Hotel or Ice Hotel, where rooms are crafted entirely from ice. Even if you don't stay overnight, exploring the hotel is a unique experience. Warm up with a traditional Lappish dinner.
