Marrakesh Morroco

Timmy makay

Posted on November 27, 2023

Jardin Majorelle: A Chill Spot If you need a break from the chaos, Jardin Majorelle is the place. It's a garden with cool blue buildings and some exotic plants. A nice change of view

Bahia Palace: We checked out Bahia Palace. It's grand, no doubt. The intricate woodwork and colorful tiles and great for pictures.

Souks: Shopping Galore The souks are a shopping paradise. Leather goods, spices, everthing. i got better at my bargaining skillss

Djemaa el Fna: Nightlife Marrakesh Style Djemaa el Fna is the main square that turns into a carnival at night. Street performers, food stalls, the whole deal. It's a bit tourist-centric, but hey, it's a spectacle.

Marrakesh is something else. Is a beautyiful city that'll leave an impression, a real adventure
