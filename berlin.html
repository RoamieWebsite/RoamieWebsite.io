Berlin Package

Start your trip by exploring the central district of Mitte. Visit the iconic Brandenburg Gate and take a stroll through Pariser Platz. Explore the Holocaust Memorial and nearby Tiergarten Park. Have dinner at a local restaurant in Mitte.

Spend the morning at Museum Island, home to several world-class museums. Don't miss the Pergamon Museum and the Neues Museum. Afternoon visit to Checkpoint Charlie and the nearby Wall Museum to learn about Berlin's Cold War history. Enjoy dinner in the lively Kreuzberg district.

Walk along the East Side Gallery, a section of the Berlin Wall adorned with vibrant street art. Explore the trendy Friedrichshain district. Visit RAW-Gelände, an alternative cultural and entertainment area. Try out local or international cuisine in the area for dinner. Spend your last day at Tierpark Berlin, one of Europe's largest zoos. Enjoy the diverse range of animals and beautiful landscapes. Have a farewell dinner at a traditional German beer hall or restaurant.
