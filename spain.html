Spain Package

Arrive in Marbella and check into your chosen accommodation, whether it's a luxurious hotel or a charming Airbnb. Spend the afternoon relaxing on the beach. Play in the Mediterranean waves, soak up the sun, and enjoy the beautiful surroundings.

After breakfast, head to the beach for a day of fun in the sun. Spend the day relaxing on the beach, swimming in the sea, and enjoying the beautiful surroundings.

Start your day with a leisurely breakfast at a local café. Explore Marbella's charming Old Town (Casco Antiguo). Wander through narrow cobblestone streets, visit the Orange Square (Plaza de los Naranjos), and discover quaint shops.
